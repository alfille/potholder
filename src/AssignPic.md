# Take a picture (Turquoise)

## Click the button

![](Photo.png)

This picture button brings up either the camera (phone) or the gallery (laptop).

Since no piece is currently selected, you will have to choose the destination:

## Choose Destination

![](Photo2.png)

The choices are either
